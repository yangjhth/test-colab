## Quick Commands

```bash
# Setup (first time only)
./setup-goal-system.sh

# Create today's plan
cp daily/TEMPLATE-daily-plan.md daily/$(date +%Y-%m-%d).md

# Open today's plan (Linux/Mac)
vim daily/$(date +%Y-%m-%d).md
# or
code daily/$(date +%Y-%m-%d).md

# View your progress
cat progress/goal-progress.md
```

## Daily Affirmations

Copy one of these into your daily plan for motivation:

- "I am building a sustainable wellness practice"
- "10 minutes of TaiChi energizes my entire day"
- "I prioritize my health and wellbeing"
- "Consistency is my superpower"
- "Each practice session brings me closer to mastery"
- "I am patient with my progress"
- "My body and mind thank me for this practice"

## Weekly Questions

Answer these in your weekly review:

1. How many days did I practice this week?
2. What time of day worked best?
3. How did I feel after practicing?
4. What obstacles came up?
5. What will I do differently next week?
6. What am I celebrating?
